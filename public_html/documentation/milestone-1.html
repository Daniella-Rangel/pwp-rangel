Milestone 1

Purpose:This project will give information on the different types of project management.This is for beginners.

Audience:This site will be designed for a professional audience. It is targeted for individuals who are interested in learning project management and applying it. Target audience people who need a base understanding of project management.

Goal: The primary goal of this site is for users to educate themselves on the basics of project management techniques. the site would go over techniques like the differences between waterfall and argyle. It will be for beginners.

Persona

Name: Samantha Smith

Profession: Samantha is a co-funder for a small local business that makes web pages for local companies. She does not know too much about project management but because her company is growing she needs to learn a new way to manage the work her employees are doing.

Technology: Uses a Mac book Pro 14 inch along with an Iphone 14 pro max with unlimited data. Her knowledge is moderate

Attitudes and Behaviors: Samantha is over worked and is in the process of hiring new developers. Because her business is expanding she needs to learn new managing techniques inorder to keep everything organized. She needs information that is fast and easy.

Frustrations and Needs: Samantha has little time to be able to take a full course on project management. She wants to learn some basics without spending a lot of time. Her company is growing rapidly, so she must be organized.

Goals:Wants a website that has straight forward information. Something that offers different styles of project management so she can find something that works for ner needs.
